Demonstration of the solution

We have created a conversational virtual assistant/bot which would help customers with queries related to their banking accounts. Users can check for their account balance, last purchase and last transfer by providing information on the information required, the account type they want the information for and their account number. The demonstration aims to showcase how the solution offers an intuitive and consistent self-service experience across multiple channels. The following sections provides details on how to experience the solution through various media.
